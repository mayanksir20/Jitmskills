Empowering India’s Youth Through Skills, Innovation & Opportunity

JITM Skills Pvt. Ltd. is one of India’s leading skill development organisations, committed to transforming lives through high-quality training, employment, and entrepreneurship. With a footprint across 20+ states, 150+ training centres, and a legacy of 1 million+ candidates trained, JITM Skills has become a trusted partner for Government missions, State Skill Missions, CSR initiatives, and corporate employers.

For over a decade, we have been delivering industry-relevant, outcome-driven training in both rural and urban areas — bridging the gap between talent and opportunity. Our programs are aligned with NSDC, MSDE, DDU-GKY, PMKVY, NULM, JSDMS, UPSDM, MAEF, , and leading PSU CSR schemes like NTPC, IOCL, NHPC & SJVN.

By focusing on real employment, practical learning, and inclusive access for underserved communities, JITM Skills is building a skilled workforce that drives India’s growth story.

Our Mission & Vision

Driving purpose through meaningful skill development, creating real impact in lives and communities, and enabling long-term transformation by combining innovation, opportunity, and industry-aligned training for a stronger future.

Our Mission

To create a skilled, confident, and future-ready workforce through high-quality training, modern digital tools, and strong industry linkages that lead to sustainable employment and entrepreneurship.

Our Vision

To be India’s most trusted and impactful skill development organization by enabling livelihoods, uplifting communities, and reducing the country’s skill gap through inclusive, outcome-driven training.
