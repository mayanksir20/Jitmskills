Healthcare & Nursing Assistant

A strong and compassionate workforce is vital to India’s healthcare system, supporting patients across hospitals, clinics, and home-care environments.

JITM Skills prepares Nursing Assistants who combine technical healthcare support with empathy, ethical responsibility, and patient-focused care.

Care Driven • Safety Focused • Professionally Trained

Core Skill Areas

Training structured around real-world healthcare responsibilities

Patient Care & Hygiene

Patient mobility assistance, hygiene maintenance, bed care, and daily patient support.

Vitals & Medical Assistance

Monitoring vital signs, charting, and assisting in basic medical procedures.

Infection Control

Sterilisation processes, infection prevention, and hospital safety practices.

Emergency & Elderly Care

Emergency response readiness, elderly patient handling, and care protocols.

Ethics & Communication

Empathy, confidentiality, ethical behaviour, and patient communication.

Career Outcomes

Multiple placement pathways across India’s healthcare ecosystem

Clinical Facilities

Hospitals, nursing homes, diagnostic centres, and rehabilitation facilities.

Home & Community Care

Home-care agencies, elderly support services, and community health programs.
