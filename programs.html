Our Comprehensive Programs

JITM Skills offers a wide range of skill development initiatives designed to empower youth, women, and marginalized communities with sustainable livelihoods.

Government Schemes

JITM Skills proudly partners with multiple Central and State Government ministries to deliver flagship skill development missions across India. These schemes focus on empowering youth from rural, urban, and marginalized communities with industry-relevant skills and employment pathways.

Our government programs are rigorously monitored, NSQF-aligned, and executed through certified training centres equipped with modern infrastructure. We ensure compliance, quality training, and high placement outcomes through dedicated teams across multiple states.

  • Pan-India implementation
  • NSQF-compliant curriculum
  • Technology-enabled training
  • Strong placement partnerships
  • Special focus on women & minorities

CSR Projects

Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) projects are a major pillar of JITM Skills. We collaborate with top Indian companies to design and implement customized skill development initiatives that focus on sustainable livelihood creation.

Our CSR programs are built around the principles of impact, scalability, transparency, and long-term socio-economic development.

  • Skill training for unemployed youth
  • Women empowerment & self-help group capacity building
  • Digital literacy initiatives
  • Vocational training aligned to local industry demand
  • Placement, apprenticeship & micro-entrepreneurship support

Our CSR partners value JITM Skills for high transparency, measurable outcomes, and community impact.

Entrepreneurship Training (EDP/ESDP)

Entrepreneurship and self-employment are essential pillars of India’s economic growth. JITM Skills delivers comprehensive Entrepreneurship Development Programs (EDP & ESDP) designed to equip participants with the skills required to launch, manage, and grow a successful business.

  • Business idea identification & validation
  • Financial literacy, budgeting & profit planning
  • Marketing, branding & customer acquisition
  • Digital tools for business development
  • One-on-one mentoring & handholding
  • Guidance for government subsidies & Mudra loans

Thousands of youth, women, and artisans have transformed into entrepreneurs through JITM’s entrepreneurship programs.

Placement & Apprenticeship Support

Employment is the true outcome of skill development, and JITM Skills ensures every trained candidate receives dedicated placement support through our national network of employers.

  • 100% placement-oriented training
  • Industry-linked apprenticeship opportunities under NAPS/NATS
  • Job fairs, campus interviews & recruitment drives
  • Interview preparation & soft skills enhancement
  • Post-placement tracking for six months

We maintain partnerships with 300+ companies in sectors like Retail, Hospitality, Healthcare, IT, Manufacturing, Logistics, Telecom, and BFSI.

Skill Development Initiatives

JITM Skills runs large-scale sector-based skilling projects supporting India’s growing workforce needs. These initiatives cater to youth, women, school dropouts, and unemployed individuals across various states.

  • National Skill Qualification Framework (NSQF) standards
  • Practical, job-oriented classroom & lab sessions
  • Industry exposure & on-the-job training
  • Soft skills, workplace readiness & English communication
  • Certification from NSDC/SSC or State Skill Missions

Our mission is to ensure every trained candidate progresses toward a sustainable livelihood.
