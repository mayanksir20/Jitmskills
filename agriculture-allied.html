Agriculture & Allied Skills

Sustainable & Profitable Agriculture Training

For millions of rural households, agriculture remains a core source of livelihood and economic stability.

JITM Skills provides modern, sustainable, and profitable agricultural training to empower farmers and rural youth with practical knowledge and income-generating skills.

Key Training Areas

Practical agricultural skills designed to improve productivity and sustainability.

Scientific Crop Production

Modern farming technologies and scientific cultivation practices.

Soil & Irrigation Management

Soil health improvement, seed treatment, and efficient irrigation methods.

Organic & Natural Farming

Organic farming techniques and natural resource conservation.

Dairy, Poultry & Fisheries

Livestock, goatery, poultry, and fisheries management skills.

Post-Harvest Techniques

Storage, processing, and value addition methods.

Agricultural Entrepreneurship

Agri-business planning, market linkage, and enterprise development.

Career Outcomes

Skills that enable sustainable income and long-term rural development.

Improved Farm Productivity

Higher yields, better crop quality, and efficient farm practices.

Agri-Allied Enterprises

Dairy, poultry, fisheries, and value-added agri ventures.

Agri-Enterprises & Cooperatives

Employment with agri-businesses, FPOs, and rural cooperatives.
